Some Fun Facts About Your Favorite Shows!

How I Met Your Mother: Theme Song

+

The theme song, "Hey Beautiful", is by the band The Solids. Both creators of the show are members of the band. Carter Bays does the vocals and guitar, and Craig Thomas plays the drums.

Breaking Bad: Jesse Pinkman Early Death

+

Jeese Pinkman was originally set to be killed off during the first season. It wasn't until episode 2, that "he, the producers, and the directors all knew Paul was a great actor to work with and that it "would be a huge, colossal mistake to kill off Jesse."

One Piece: Popularity

+

The world renowned manga and anime, One Piece, is the top most sold manga in history.

The Office: Famous Hollywood Directors

+

J.J. Abrams directed the episode 'Cocktails', Jon Favreau directed the episode 'Moving On', Joss Whedon directed the episodes 'Branch Wars' and 'Business School'.
